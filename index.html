It's Not a Pipeline.
It's a Spiral.

Capture, Development, and Exposure aren't three sequential phases. They're three activities that happen together at every maturity level.

At Level 0, you capture a signal, shape it in two sentences, and expose it to an AI. At Level 3, you capture feedback, reshape a draft, and run it past a stakeholder informally.

The biggest gain? Building the exposure reps at Levels 2–3 — the informal, low-stakes, inquiry-framed sharing that Wayne calls "socializing." That's the missing muscle.
